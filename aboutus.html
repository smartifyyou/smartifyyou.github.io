About Us

Smartifyyou: Your Hostel or PG is Now Your EV Scooter Hub

Smartifyou is a website and WhatsApp-based service that makes hostels and PGs into EV scooter rental hubs. This means that residents of hostels and PGs can now rent EV scooters on an hourly and daily basis, making it easy and convenient to get around town without having to worry about traffic or parking.

Anyone over the age of 18 with a valid driver's licence can rent an EV scooter from Smartifyyou. All of our EV scooters come with a charger. You can charge the scooter at any standard electrical outlet

Our Vision

Our vision is clear: To empower residents of hostels and PGs with easy access to electric scooters parked right on your premises, enhancing your daily commute and making a positive impact on the environment. We want to create a future where sustainability meets seamless mobility.

How It Works

  • Check availability: Check the booking status of the scooter in status of the official Whatsapp account of SmartifyYou.
  • Send a Booking Request: If the scooter is free, send a booking request message over WhatsApp. Our chat assistant will guide you further.
  • Complete the Payment: Pay for our ride and you are ready to ride!
  • Unlock and Ride: Enjoy your ride once you complete the payment. Your ride will end only when you park the scooter back at its designated location from where you picked it.
