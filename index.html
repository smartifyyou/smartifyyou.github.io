Our Services

Discover the ease of commuting with our electric scooter rental services tailored for hostels and PGs. Our EV Scooters are stationed right at your residence, providing hassle-free access for both hourly and daily rides.

STEP 1

Check availability
Check the booking status of the scooter in status of the official Whatsapp account of SmartifyYou.

STEP 2

Send a Booking Request
If the scooter is free, send a booking request message over WhatsApp. Our chat assistant will guide you further.

STEP 3

Complete the Payment
Pay for our ride and you are ready to ride!

STEP 4

Unlock and Ride
Enjoy your ride once you complete the payment. Your ride will end only when you park the scooter back at its designated location from where you picked it.
